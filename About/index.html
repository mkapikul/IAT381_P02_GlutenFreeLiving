About Us

Who we are, and what we do?

“Gluten Free Living” website seeks to provide anyone considering a gluten-free lifestyle the knowledge and confidence to embrace this bountiful life.

From the nutritionist counseling patients about living gluten-free, to the empowered consumer managing his or her own gluten-free world, by bringing together, credible research, recipes and gluten free products to be purchased into a single, easy-to-digest resource.

We offer a wide range of products for people with different food sensitivities, one-click option to buy recipe ingredients and Canada wide free express shipping on all purchases regardless of dollar amount.

For further infomrmations, plese contact our corporate office.
