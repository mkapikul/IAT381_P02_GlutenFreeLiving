We are under new management

Today we are proudly announcing the appointment of Linda Walsh as Senior Vice President/General Manager.

As the new SVP/GM, Walsh will lead all marketing, new product development and strategic growth efforts to continue to build upon Gluten Free Living's success as a leading gluten-free lifestyle brand.

Walsh will take the place of Isak McWalter, former Vice President/General Manager of Gluten Free Living, who had been with the company for more than five years.

“I am excited to join a brand that is a true pioneer and innovator in the gluten-free space,” said Walsh, the new Senior Vice President/General Manager. “I am highly impressed with company leadership and vision and look forward to elevating the brand to the next level.”

With more than 10 years of experience working in the natural products, food and wellness industries, Walsh is perfect fit to lead Gluten Free living. Walsh has an extensive background in marketing and general management, with brands such as Silk Soymilk. She was also a co-founder of Pixie Mate, a yerba mate company, and has worked as the Vice President/General Manager of Glutino’s sister company Earth Balance since 2009.
